Group's First Anniversary

Published on July 30, 2024 by Alexandre Giron

The "Grupo de Pesquisa Criptografia Pós-Quântica - UTFPR" was created in March, 2023. The primary goal is to gather people interested in studying concepts, applications and adoption strategies, focusing on the development and evaluation of Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC). Other topics also belongs to the group's interest. Being the group's coordinator, I would like to thank to the hero students that have been studying, attending meetings and starting research in order to achieve the group's primary goal. After one year of activities, we achieved some partnerships that are (hopefully!) promising! Some results: Obviously we are still at the earliest stage. But we hope to contribute and we welcome new collaborations!
